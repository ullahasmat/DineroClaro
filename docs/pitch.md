# DineroClaro - 1 minute pitch

- Problem: Financial guidance is complex and inaccessible for first-time investors and gig workers.
- Solution: DineroClaro delivers bilingual, bite-sized guidance with an AI coach and actionable next steps.
- Demo: Ask a money question, get a tailored plan, track a checklist, and receive Supabase-backed progress.
- Why now: OpenAI models + open banking APIs make personalized advice feasible in a weekend build.
- Call to action: Try the demo, then share feedback on the clarity of recommendations.
